# RayData: The Power of Railway Data

## The RayData Story

A data revolution rising from Istanbul... The story of numbers dancing over the network of thousands of kilometers belonging to Turkey's largest railway operator. Every trip, every ticket, every kilometer... All coming together to shape the future of railway transportation.

**RayData** is not just a platform; it's the symbol of our big step toward understanding and optimizing railway operations.

---

## Why RayData?

In the age of data, information is power. **RayData** brings exactly this power to the railway sector:

- **Ray** (Rail): The steel network of railways, thousands of trains, millions of passengers, the network reaching loads
- **Data**: The meaning of every trip, every ticket, every kilometer, numbers transforming into decision-making processes

This name reminds us: Making decisions without data is like traveling in the dark. With RayData, we come into the light.

---

## Our Story

TCDD Transportation is Turkey's largest railway transportation company. From high-speed trains (YHT) carrying thousands of passengers between cities every day to freight trains transporting goods to all corners of the country... All this movement generates data.

However, this data was stored in scattered systems, in different formats, and could not be sufficiently integrated into decision-making processes. This is where **RayData** was born.

### A Vision Starting from Istanbul

The heart of Istanbul, one of the technology centers... In this city, as the **PiA Group** team, we are working to contribute to revealing the power of railway data.

From passenger transportation to freight operations, from fleet maintenance to safety management, from resource planning to real-time tracking... In every area, data would become the center of decision-making processes.

### The Need from Ankara

Ankara, the capital of Turkey. TCDD Transportation's decision center is here. Operations managers, strategy team, financial analysts... All needed better data, faster reports, more in-depth analyses.

**RayData**, which we developed from Istanbul, empowers decision-makers in Ankara. It enables them to see, understand, and optimize operations thousands of kilometers away in real-time.

## RayData's Vision

RayData is not just a BI platform; it's the digital brain of railway operations. Every dashboard, every report, every KPI card contributes to the sector's data-driven transformation.

### Data Excellence

Modern data warehouse architecture, OLAP cube structures, real-time data streams... RayData offers a scalable, fast, and reliable analytics platform using the most current state of technology that transforms data into meaningful information.

### Visualization Power

From executive dashboards to operational monitoring panels, from in-depth analytical reports to predictive insights... Users at every level see the information they need in the right format.

Where is the Istanbul-Ankara high-speed train right now? How many trains are delayed today? Which station has congestion? RayData enables the operations team to see the instant status with real-time data streams.

## RayData's Features

### 3 Comprehensive Dashboards

**Executive Dashboard**: Strategic KPIs, trends, comparative analyses... Summary view for top management.

**Operations Dashboard**: Real-time train tracking, live alerts, daily operational metrics... Instant status for the operations team.

**Analytics Dashboard**: Predictive models, in-depth analyses, OLAP cubes... Detailed examination for data scientists.

### 100 Detailed Reports

Passenger transportation, freight operations, maintenance management, safety, resource planning, financial performance... Comprehensive reports in every area. 20 complex reports with artificial intelligence integration, 80 standard reports for quick access.

### Realistic Simulation with Synthetic Data

RayData was tested with 12-36 months of synthetic data before real data integration. Seasonal patterns, realistic train trips, statistically robust distributions... Everything was designed to simulate real scenarios.

## Technical Excellence

**RayData** was built with a modern technology stack:

- **Frontend**: React-based, responsive, accessible user interface
- **Data Visualization**: Interactive charts with Recharts, geographic maps with Leaflet
- **Data Warehouse**: Star schema architecture, OLAP cubes, ETL pipelines
- **Real-Time**: Live data streaming with WebSocket, event streaming with Kafka
- **Artificial Intelligence**: Demand forecasts, failure predictions, optimization algorithms
- **Open Source**: Open source technologies like PostgreSQL, Redis, Elasticsearch
